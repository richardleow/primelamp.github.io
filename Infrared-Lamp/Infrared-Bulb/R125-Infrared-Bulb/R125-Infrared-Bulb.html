So this Infrared Bulb is widedly used home and abroad.

Why others disappoint….
Conventional Infrared Bulbs are cheap but they fail within a few months. Why?

1. The base breaks away from bulb easier.
2. The glass degrades (duo to the heat).
3. The glass and filament are so fragile that a minimal impact shatters them.

Prime's bulbs, by intentional design, address all 3 issues. Yes, they cost more $$ to initially buy but they are cheaper long term because they last years instead only a few weeks.

Bulb comparision – Prime's new bulb VS other's typical bulbs.
never-loose base on our new bulbs.

Other bulb.
Attached with cement adhesive. Heat causes the cement to dry and lose its adhesive property. The base breaks away from the bulb.
